1. Introduction
Welcome to Ultimate Flaggle ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we use cookies and what information we store.

2. What Data We Collect
We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses. However, we use a randomly generated unique ID to track your win streak and other game stats.

3. How We Use Cookies
We store a cookie in your browser containing a unique ID. This allows us to:

Track your win streak and game stats
Improve your user experience by remembering your progress
This cookie is not shared with third parties and does not contain personally identifiable information.

4. Managing Cookies
If you do not want this cookie stored in your browser, you can:

Disable cookies in your browser settings
Clear cookies manually from your browser
However, disabling cookies may prevent the app from tracking your progress correctly.

5. Third-Party Services
We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertisements.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
